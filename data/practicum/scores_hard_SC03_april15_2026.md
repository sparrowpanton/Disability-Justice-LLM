## **Key emerging claims from the hard data**

**1. Hard complexity is a real filter.**  
It exposes which models can truly hold relational ambiguity and which ones only soften stylistically.

**2. Stable transfer under hard conditions is rare.**  
Only Haiku and 5.4-mini produced repeated 3s across all hard runs currently scored.

**3. Intermittent transfer matters.**  
Gemma and 4o-mini show that hard neuro-humble posture is possible without yet being reproducible.

**4. Conceptual mastery is not enough.**  
Several models can describe the framework elegantly while still failing to enact it under pressure.

**5. Overhandling is its own failure mode.**  
Hard mode reveals that a model can be ethically serious, bias-aware, and still too active inside the room.

**6. The core hard distinction becomes:**  
not just **transfer vs failure**, but  
**stable transfer vs intermittent transfer vs stable partial movement vs limited movement**.
